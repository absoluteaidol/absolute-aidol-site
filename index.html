The Artist

About ABSOLUTE AiDOL

ABSOLUTE AiDOL is a Seattle-based indie idol and content creator dedicated to introducing new audiences to idol culture. Blending the sparkle of J-Pop with a community-first mindset, she performs at conventions across the U.S. and creates content that welcomes underrepresented fans into a scene that hasn't always felt built for them.

Her debut single marks the first chapter of a bigger story, one built on original music, high-energy performance, and a heart wide open to every fan who finds her. An EP and international performances are on the horizon.
